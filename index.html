About Me

Hello! My name is Paul and I'm a full stack web developer. I love to solve problems and write code. My strengths lie in my ability to think critically and solve new problems. I'm excited to join a team that allows me to create interesting projects and grow as a developer.

I started programming after taking an intro to CS class in college. Building simple apps in Python was enough to push me to pursue a career in programming. I felt particularly drawn to the creative aspects of web development, and still love learning new tools and frameworks to build applications.

I recently graduated from an immersive web development bootcamp, where I built four full stack web apps. Here I spent many hours collaborating with other developers, and building projects to strengthen my understanding of programming.
